A natural gel soap helping to accelerate the exfoliation of skin

A luxurious homemade body soap which can be also be used on the face and hands. A gentle soap rich in vitamin E helping to moisturise the body without affecting the body's microbiome. Best used with an exfoliating mitt to turnover and remove dead skin cells giving an all over fresh and oxygenated experience. Leaving you feeling so good you will want to manifest your dreams in the bathroom. How to use: As a daily cleanser: massage a good pinch of gel all over the body and rinse off with water. Ideally with a good exfoliating mitt. As a body softening masque: apply a generous amount after spending 5 minutes in the bath or steam room. Leave on for up to 5 minutes and remove dead skin cells with an exfoliating mitt. Warning: Avoid contact with eyes. If product gets into eyes rinse immediately with warm water.
